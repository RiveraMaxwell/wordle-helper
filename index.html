How to Use

1. Type your guess into the boxes.

2. Right-click a box to change its color:
green = correct spot   yellow = wrong spot   gray = not in word

3. Click Find to see all possible words.

4. Click any word in the results to fill the boxes. Green boxes stay locked.

Random — shows 10 random suggestions, preferring all-different-letter words.

Reset — clears everything and restores the full word pool.

Letter Heatmap — shows how often each letter appears in the remaining pool after Find. Darker = more common.

Best Guess — ranks top suggestions by how many pool words they'd eliminate (entropy scoring). Appears after Find.

On mobile: long-press a box to change color.
